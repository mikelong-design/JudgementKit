# Social Media Image Render Style Context

## Shorthand prompt phrase
"Foggy Bloom Editorial"

## Core style descriptor
Soft, foggy, desaturated enterprise editorial illustration that mixes photoreal base forms with painterly airbrushed treatment. Calm, optimistic mood with diffused warm bloom over a blue-gray palette, minimal linework, subtle film grain, and floating dust motes.

## Visual priorities
1. Clarity at a glance, with a single conceptual centerpiece.
2. Low visual noise, with large negative space and soft edge control.
3. Mood-first lighting, with warm bloom as the focal signal and cool ambient haze everywhere else.

## Palette and tone
1. Base palette: blue-gray, slate, cool neutrals, low saturation.
2. Highlight palette: warm white to pale gold, used only for focal glow and small spark points.
3. Contrast: value contrast over hue contrast. Avoid deep blacks and harsh whites.

## Lighting and atmosphere
1. Primary light: diffused, high-key bloom near the concept focal point.
2. Atmosphere: volumetric haze and soft falloff across the frame.
3. Particles: subtle bokeh or dust motes that suggest activity without clutter.

## Rendering and texture
1. Airbrushed gradients with very soft transitions.
2. Minimal hard edges, with atmospheric perspective to separate depth planes.
3. Fine film grain or paper-like noise to reduce digital sharpness.
4. Selective depth of field, with the focal area slightly clearer than the rest.

## Linework and iconography
1. Minimal linework overall.
2. Thin outline accents only where needed for legibility of interface symbols.
3. Rounded, simplified UI glyphs and geometric elements.
4. Avoid detailed technical diagrams, dense UI, or sharp vector styling.

## Composition language
1. Center-weighted glow with generous negative space.
2. Layered depth planes: foreground context, midground concept object, background faint UI or environment.
3. Subjects should feel secondary to the concept centerpiece.
4. Avoid busy scenes, crowded collages, and high-frequency detail.

## Motifs that fit well
1. Organic propagation: dandelion filaments, drifting seeds, branching forms, soft networks.
2. Systems metaphors: gentle paths, flowing contours, floating nodes, subtle interface tiles.
3. Quiet human presence: silhouettes or softly detailed people, understated and non-dominant.

## Motifs to avoid
1. Cyberpunk neon, saturated gradients, or high-contrast sci-fi lighting.
2. Heavy outlines, comic styling, or sharp flat-vector poster looks.
3. Overly literal robots, aggressive futurism, or dystopian tone.
4. Text inside the image, unless explicitly requested.

## Prompt template
Use this block at the end of any image prompt.
